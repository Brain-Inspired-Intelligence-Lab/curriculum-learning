Workshop Scope

This workshop introduces Curriculum Learning as a practical strategy for improving training stability, sample efficiency, and model generalization. Participants will learn how to design curricula that sequence data and tasks from easy to hard, and how to adapt schedules based on loss dynamics.

  1. Core Concepts – foundations, intuition, and empirical evidence.
  2. Scheduling Strategies – self-paced learning, teacher-student curricula, and adaptive sampling.
  3. Evaluation – metrics for stability, convergence, and generalization.
  4. Applications – NLP, CV, and multi-modal training pipelines.

Hands-on examples will demonstrate how curriculum design can reduce training time and improve model robustness.
